Hot spots

Parthenon

    The Parthenon is a one of the most
    famous historical sites in the world.
    It is one of the greatest creations by
    the Greek democracy in 490 BC but was
    destoried in 480 BC by the Persian so
    only the remains are left.

Acropolis of Athens

    The Acropolis of Athens
    is one of the most famous
    ancient archaeological sites
    in the world.Located on a
    limestone hill high above Athens,
    Greece, the Acropolis has been
    inhabited since prehistoric times.

Syntagma Square

    "Constitution Square" is the central
    square of Athens. The square is named
    after the Constitution that Otto, the
    first King of Greece, was obliged to
    grant after a popular and military
    uprising on 3 September 1843.
