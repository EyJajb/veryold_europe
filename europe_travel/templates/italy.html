Hot spots

St. Mark's Basilica

    St. Mark's Basilica, aka
    the Patriarchal Cathedral
    Basilica of Saint Mark, is the
    captures the true essence of
    italo-byzantine architecture
    and is considered one of the
    most important religious
    buildings in Northern Italy.

Ponti di Rialto

    The Ponte di Rialto connects
    the San Marco and San Polo
    districts of Venice and is
    one of the most iconic places
    to visit in italy, with its
    beautiful and simple design
    that compliments the grand
    canal.

Palazzo Ducale

    The Palazzo Ducale, aka Doge's
    Palace,is a palce built in
    Venetian Gothic style and
    used to be the home of the
    Doge, the ruler of Venice.
    Contains many rooms filled
    with beautiful ornate
    ceilings and fireplaces.
